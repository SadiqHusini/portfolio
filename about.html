ABOUT ME

My Background

I’m a 23-year-old student currently studying Graphic Design, where I get to explore my creativity and passion for visual storytelling. My studies allow me to experiment with different design techniques and tools, pushing me to improve my skills every day. I love how design can communicate emotions and ideas without needing words—it’s like creating a visual language. Alongside school, I’m always looking for ways to apply what I learn in real-world projects, both for fun and for building my portfolio.

Gaming is one of my biggest hobbies, and Fortnite is my go-to game. I love the thrill of strategizing, competing, and teaming up with friends online. It’s more than just entertainment for me; it’s a way to unwind and sometimes even spark creative ideas for my designs. My passion for gaming also ties into another of my hobbies: making YouTube videos. Whether it’s gameplay highlights, tutorials, or creative montages, I enjoy sharing my gaming adventures with others.

A big part of my YouTube process involves creating thumbnail pictures. Thumbnails are the first thing people see, and I’m always striving to make mine stand out. It’s like a mini design challenge every time—how do I grab someone’s attention in just a fraction of a second? This skill has helped me improve my photo editing and taught me the importance of visual impact in design.

Outside of YouTube, I spend a lot of time working on photo editing. I enjoy taking ordinary pictures and transforming them into something extraordinary through color correction, effects, and creative compositions. Whether it’s for personal projects, assignments, or helping a friend with their photos, editing has become a big part of how I express myself artistically.

Beyond gaming and design, I’m passionate about exploring new tools and technologies. I’m fascinated by how evolving tech continues to shape the creative industry, and I love learning new software or techniques to stay ahead. In the future, I hope to combine my love for gaming, YouTube, and graphic design into a career that lets me do what I love every day while constantly challenging myself to grow.

Skills & Experience

I’m skilled in Photoshop, where I excel at photo editing, creating stunning visuals, and designing custom thumbnails. In Figma, I specialize in crafting user-friendly interfaces and prototyping designs that bring ideas to life. Additionally, I have a solid foundation in user research, allowing me to understand user needs and translate them into impactful designs that prioritize functionality and aesthetics.
